# Business Understanding
- Framework used to understand Business
    - Macro -> Micro = High levels -> Low levels = Big picture -> Details

1. **Macro - Macroeconomy**
    - Context: The business within the broader economic environment.
        - Goal: Understand how external economic conditions impact the operations of the business.
    - Dimension:
        - Time: Historical trends, seasonal patterns, cyclical variations
        - Geography: Regional differences, global markets
        - Industry: Sector-specific trends, competitive landscape
        - Technology: Digital transformation, automation, innovation
        - Development Stage: Market Maturity
        - Cultural Factors: Social norms, values, beliefs
        - Economic Factors: Income levels, employment rates, consumer spending
        - Social Factors: Demographics, lifestyle changes, social trends
        - Environmental Factors: Climate change, sustainability, environmental regulations

2. **Micro - Microeconomy**
    - Context: The business within the company's internal environment.
        - Goal: Understand how internal factors impact the operations of the business.
    - Dimension:
        - Time: Historical trends, seasonal patterns, cyclical variations
        - Geography: Market location, regional differences
        - Industry: Market dynamics, competition, regulations
        - Business Model: Revenue streams, cost structure, value proposition
        - Stakeholders: Customers, suppliers, regulators, investors
        - Technology: Digital transformation, automation, innovation
        - Brand: Company reputation, brand identity, brand perception
        - Business Owner: Owner's vision, goals, and objectives
        - Operations: Day-to-day business activities, processes, and procedures
        ---
        - Culture (Optional): Organizational values, work environment, employee engagement
        - Leadership (Optional): Management style, decision-making processes, strategic vision
        - Risk Management (Optional): Risk assessment, mitigation strategies, crisis response
        - Performance Management (Optional): KPIs, metrics, performance reviews
        - Change Management (Optional): Adaptation to change, innovation, continuous improvement
        - Communication (Optional): Internal and external communication channels
        - Collaboration (Optional): Teamwork, partnerships, stakeholder engagement
        - Innovation (Optional): New ideas, products, services, processes
        - Sustainability (Optional): Environmental, social, and governance (ESG) considerations
        - Ethics (Optional): Moral principles, corporate responsibility, transparency
        - Trust (Optional): Building relationships, credibility, reputation
        - Reputation (Optional): Brand image, public perception, customer loyalty
